Welcome to our Human Computer Interaction Experiment

Our experiment will be conducted in the following order:

Enter Your Personal Information

We need you to answer few demographic questions.

Sample Question

You will be able to solve some sample question to get a feel of the real quiz.

Connect Muse

Muse is a device that read brain waves, experimenter will help you with Muse connection.

Take Quiz

We would need you to take a Quiz after answering few Math Questions.

Take a Break

We need you to relax for few minutes before continuing the experiment.

Listen to Music

We would need you to close your eyes, relax and we would play music for 10 minutes.

Take Quiz Again

We would need you to take a similar Quiz again.
